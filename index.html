Who We Are

We are the soul behind the majestic mountains, mystic rivers, beloved tributaries, unadulterated air, green expanse and organic soil that make Uttarakhand an object of affection worldwide. We are the sons and daughters of the pious Devbhoomi who have taken it upon themselves to spread the wonders of Uttarakhand’s way of life to the world one step at a time.

Every product is a meticulous result of love, dedication and devotion to the almighty. What makes us different is that we would never compromise on the health of our brethren. Our products are harvested from the earth organically, designed with our hands and packaged with utmost care. We implore you to go natural with us and leave the world a better place for the next generation.
