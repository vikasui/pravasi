About Us

Modern lifestyle has taken a toll. It is disastrously affecting the environment, our natural rhythm and life cycle. It is poisoning the food we eat, water we drink and products we consume. The things we hold most dearest to our heart have been industrialised. The natural scent has been replaced by chemicals The market for incense is one of the most disturbing examples of our quest for modernisation. Not only is this bad for humans, it also discredits our devotion to the almighty. Anything impure should not any place in our homes, hearts or prayers. Chemically produced incense is in no way an answer to our growing needs. Some people blame it on the need for cheaper prices, but that is completely incorrect.

We are PravasiVikas have travelled back to our roots to bring back the fragrance that used to envelop our prayers. Our products are a result of backbreaking research, and a common sense approach. The air we breathe cannot be poisoned with chemically laden incense and neither can our devotion be compromised. Our products are tested to the limits and hold an authenticity that is unmatched in the market. Our products are better, superior in every way possible. They are compositely made of natural ingredients and worked on with bare hands. If you remember the rare smell of nature, the beauty of its offerings, then you will know what makes our incense products a worldwide favourite. We owe ourselves to change our way of living, and this is one step in that direction. We request to join our family in making the world a safer place to live.
